Math 363 Learning Outcomes and Homework


Homework Policy

Homework assignments for each learning outcome, consisting of problems from the required textbook, will be posted below throughout the semester. Some questions are for turning in, and some are for extra practice. For a given assignment, you should submit all ‘turn-in’ problems, and feedback on your solutions will be provided by me or the TA. If you submit an incorrect or incomplete solution, you will have the opportunity to resubmit for full credit. All homework papers should be neatly written, stapled, and free from perforated note- book holes. Any assignment not in this condition will not be accepted. Satisfactorily completing 80% of homework assignments will earn you an M in LO29.

Solutions

  • LO1: I can describe sample spaces and/or events by listing all simple events they contain.
  • LO2: I can use properties of unions, intersections, and/or complements to find the probability of compound events.
  • LO3: I can calculate probabilities of events using counting techniques.
  • LO4: I can employ the techniques of conditional probability to find the probability of events.
  • LO5: I can determine if two events are independent and/or disjoint.
  • LO6: I can use probability rules and axioms to prove statements about probability or independence.
