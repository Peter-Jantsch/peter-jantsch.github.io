Math 363 Learning Outcomes and Homework


Homework Policy

Homework assignments for each learning outcome, consisting of problems from the required textbook, will be posted below throughout the semester. Some questions are for turning in, and some are for extra practice. For a given assignment, you should submit all ‘turn-in’ problems, and feedback on your solutions will be provided by me or the TA. If you submit an incorrect or incomplete solution, you will have the opportunity to resubmit for full credit. All homework papers should be neatly written, stapled, and free from perforated note- book holes. Any assignment not in this condition will not be accepted. Satisfactorily completing 80% of homework assignments will earn you an M in LO29.

Among the problems you turn in, I will ask the TA to do a few things:

  • Grade a subset of 4-6 of the problems. If you are missing a crucial piece, or don’t seem to understand the concepts, the TA will make a comment and will not get credit for that problem.
  • Check to see that you did all the problems. Ungraded problems that you turned in are graded on effort only.
  • On the front of the homework, you will get “Satisfactory” (S) or “Not Satisfactory” (NS). If you get NS, there will be a comment about what you should do to get a satisfactory score (e.g., "finish all problems", "correct #2.13", etc.). In general, you will just need to redo problems you didn’t complete or missed to earn a satisfactory score. Please redo problems on a new sheet of paper, and turn in your old assignment with the corrected work.
