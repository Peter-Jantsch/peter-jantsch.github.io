Math 263 Homework


Homework Policy

Homework assignments consisting of problems from the required textbook will be posted below throughout the semester. Some questions are for turning in, and some are for extra practice. For a given assignment, you should submit all ‘turn-in’ problems, and feedback on your solutions will be provided by me or the TA. If you submit an incorrect or incomplete solution, you will have the opportunity to resubmit for full credit. All homework papers should be neatly written, stapled, and free from perforated note- book holes. Any assignment not in this condition will not be accepted.
