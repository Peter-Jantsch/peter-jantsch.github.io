Syllabus and Course Policies


Syllabus

Working Together

Feel free to share your code with lab and project partners. You should not directly share code with anyone outside of your group. You may discuss problems, errors, and strategies outside of your group, but should not show or share your code---figuring out the answers for yourself is a major part of the course!

Makeups

Makeups for homework assignments are only permitted under extenuating circumstances if proper documentation provided within one week of the missed assessment. Extenuating circumstances include illness, family emergencies, and College-sanctioned athletic or academic events. Makeup quizzes will not be permitted, but I will drop 3 quiz grades through the semester. If you are missing class frequently for any reason, please come talk to me. If you miss a lab session, you may makeup the work by turning in the completed assignment by the due date. However, you will have to work without a lab partner, though you may still ask for help from me or the TA.
